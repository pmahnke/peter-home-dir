Free Installation

One day, on site installation is available at no charge. Installation includes:

  • Installing the software on your computer(s)
  • Entering at least some of your data, at least enough to do some charge calculations
  • Instruction on how to use the program for as many of your people as you wish
  • Answers to any questions you might have

There is no charge for the installation except out of pocket expenses:

  • Travel expense
  • Lodging ( if required )
  • Meals
