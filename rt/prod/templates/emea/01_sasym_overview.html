Gartner Symposium/ITxpo Africa 2002
"Now is the Time to Take Advantage of the Economic Downturn - Survival of the Fittest"

5-7 August 2002, Johannesburg

In more than 30 presentations (grouped under Environment, Strategy, Customers, Partners and Architecture), Gartner analysts provided advice on how to overcome the obstacles created by the current economic climate and use the "gap year" to prepare businesses for the predicted up-turn. The keynote address was delivered by Steve Prentice on Emerging Trends and Technologies.
